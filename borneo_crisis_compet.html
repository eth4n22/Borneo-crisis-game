DDT Decision: Borneo Crisis

Multiplayer Systems Policy Game

Turn-based simulation based on the Borneo DDT/cat drop story. Each player manages their own crisis for 12 turns. Make strategic decisions to control disease while managing ecological consequences.

📖 How to Play

← Back to Start

🎯 Objective

Get the highest score (0-100) by controlling disease while keeping the ecosystem balanced. You have 12 turns — make them count!

🔄 How the Game Works

  • 2–4 players take turns in round-robin style
  • Each turn represents 2 months (12 turns = 2 years total)
  • Choose ONE action per turn
  • Your decisions affect disease, animals, trust, and budget

🎮 Your Four Actions

💧 A) Spray DDT Cost: 12

Kills mosquitoes quickly and reduces malaria. But beware — it also harms cats, which can cause rats to multiply and spread more disease later.

⚠️ Effects are delayed by 2 turns

⏸️ B) Do Nothing Cost: Free

Saves your budget, but mosquitoes will breed and disease may spread. Your trust with the community also drops slightly.

🐱 C) Cat Drop Cost: 15

Restores cat populations to hunt rats. More cats = fewer rats = less disease. But it's expensive!

⚠️ Effects are delayed by 1 turn

🐀 D) Rat Campaign Cost: 10

Quickly reduces rat population and disease. However, it may accidentally harm some cats too.

⚠️ Effects are delayed by 1 turn

⚠️ What to Watch Out For

  • Delayed Effects: Actions don't work immediately. DDT takes 2 turns to show ecological impact.
  • Balance is Key: Overusing one action creates problems. DDT kills cats → rats explode → disease surges!
  • Trust Matters: If trust drops below 25, your actions become 30% less effective.
  • Budget Limits: If budget goes negative, you can't afford paid actions until you recover to 10+.
  • Eco Collapse: If cats drop below 20 AND rats exceed 80, it's ecological collapse — major score penalty!

🏆 Winning Tips

  • Lower deaths — biggest factor in your score
  • Keep disease under control — don't let infections peak
  • Avoid ecological collapse — maintain cat/rat balance
  • Preserve trust and budget — they give bonus points
  • Think ahead — remember delayed effects!

📊 Advanced Rules & Exact Values ▼

🎯 Scoring Formula

Score = 100

− Deaths penalty: deaths × 0.6 (max −60)

− Peak penalty: peak_infection × 0.1 (max −20)

− Duration penalty: outbreak_turns × 1.5 (max −15)

− Eco penalty: eco_collapse × 5 (max −20)

+ Trust bonus: floor(trust/10) (max +10)

+ Budget bonus: floor(budget/20) (max +5)
