MISSION BRIEFING

HOW TO PLAY

🎯 Objective

Get the highest score (0–100) by managing disease control while protecting your village across 12 turns. Balance disease, ecosystem, housing, food, budget, and community trust for the best outcome.

🔄 Turn Flow

  • Start of Turn: Auto-receive +3 budget. Chose +10 last turn? Gain a +3 investment bonus too.
  • Auto-Draw: One card drawn automatically at turn start.
  • Choose One: Take +10 Budget now, or draw 1 extra card. Not both.
  • Play Cards: Click to select cards from your hand. Multiple cards can be queued.
  • Confirm: Press Confirm Actions to resolve your turn, then Next Player.

Hand limit: 6 cards. Most cards cost budget.

🏘️ Key Mechanics

  • Village Health — Main survival metric. Zero = collapse.
  • Mosquitoes — Drive malaria infection rate.
  • Rats — Spread plague and consume grain.
  • Cats — Naturally hunt rats. DDT harms cats indirectly.
  • Wasps — Control caterpillars. DDT kills wasps.
  • Caterpillars — Eat thatch, degrading roof integrity.
  • Roof & Grain — Low values inflict direct health harm each turn.
  • Public Trust — At 70+, village gains a +1 health recovery per turn.
  • Budget — Currency for playing cards. Runs out fast.

💡 Strategy Tips

  • Plan Ahead: DDT ecology harm takes 2 turns. Cat drops take 1. Budget your patience.
  • Don't Spray Blind: DDT kills mosquitoes but triggers a cascade — fewer wasps, more caterpillars, weaker cats, more rats, more plague.
  • Roof & Grain Matter: They are invisible threats. Below 50, they cause direct health loss every single turn.
  • Invest Early: Choosing +10 budget grants a +3 bonus next turn — compound it.
  • Trust Is Armour: High trust gives a passive health recovery. Let it slip and you lose that buffer.

🏆 Scoring

Score (0–100) penalises: deaths (max −60), peak infection (max −20), outbreak duration (max −15), ecological collapse events (max −20), and collapsed roof/grain (−5 each). Bonuses from trust and remaining budget.
